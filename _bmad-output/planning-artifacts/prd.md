# Product Requirements Document - solo-aaa-agency

**Author:** Michael

## Executive Summary

solo-aaa-agency is an AI-powered solo agency revolutionizing how small businesses access and integrate AI solutions. Unlike generic AI platforms that fail to address specific business needs, solo-aaa-agency provides personalized consultation, custom AI agents, and automation solutions that seamlessly integrate into existing business tools and workflows.

The agency targets small businesses (5-20 employees) that struggle to understand how AI can solve their specific problems. These businesses lack the time and expertise to integrate AI into their daily operations, and existing solutions are too generic or require costly migration.

The core value proposition: **"L'IA qui s'adapte à votre entreprise, pas l'inverse"** - Zero migration required, seamless integration into existing tools, and ROI that funds business growth through liberated budget reallocation.

The agency's unique advantage lies in using the same proven AI solutions internally that it sells to clients, creating a self-improving, scalable business model targeting $100M in revenue.

### What Makes This Special

The moment of revelation for users occurs during the live demonstration combined with visualizing savings through simulation. This "Woah" moment happens when:

1. **Live Demonstration**: Clients see AI working in real-time within their specific business context (automated phone calls, email management, appointment scheduling), adapted to their industry sector.

2. **Savings Simulation**: The concrete visualization of potential savings (20,000€-26,000€/year liberated per automated role) transforms abstraction into tangible reality. Clients immediately understand the ROI and possible budget reallocation.

3. **Zero-Migration Integration**: The promise "L'IA qui s'adapte à votre entreprise, pas l'inverse" materializes - no migration, no interruption, connection to existing tools.

4. **Personalized Consultation**: The AI-guided discovery process, with human escalation available, ensures deep understanding of needs before solution design.

5. **Proven Solutions**: The agency uses internally the same solutions it sells, ensuring reliability and continuous improvement.

6. **Positive Economic Messaging**: Focus on "liberation" and "reallocation" rather than "replacement" - transforming team members into strategic advisors rather than replacement.

## SaaS B2B Specific Requirements

### Project-Type Overview

solo-aaa-agency is a B2B SaaS platform built with Next.js, designed to serve multiple clients (tenants) from a single application instance. The platform provides AI-powered automation solutions for small businesses, with multi-user access, role-based permissions, and extensive third-party integrations.

### Technical Architecture Considerations

#### Multi-Tenancy Architecture

**Architecture Model: Schema Per Tenant**

- **Single Application Instance**: One Next.js application serves all tenants
- **Single Database**: One database instance hosts all tenant data
- **Schema Isolation**: Each client (tenant) has its own database schema for complete data isolation
- **Benefits**: 
  - Efficient resource utilization
  - Simplified application deployment and maintenance
  - Complete data isolation between tenants
  - Scalable architecture supporting growth

**Implementation Considerations:**
- Tenant identification through subdomain or tenant ID in requests
- Schema routing middleware to direct queries to correct tenant schema
- Tenant provisioning process to create new schemas for new clients
- Schema migration strategy for updates across all tenants

#### Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)

## Project Scoping & Phased Development

### MVP Strategy & Philosophy

**MVP Approach: Problem-Solving MVP**

The MVP focuses on solving the core problem with minimal features: enabling small businesses to access and integrate AI solutions without migration, delivering immediate value through automation of repetitive tasks.

**Core Problem Statement:**
Small businesses struggle to understand how AI can solve their specific problems. Existing solutions are too generic, require migration, or lack personalized consultation. They need a solution that adapts to their existing tools and workflows.

**MVP Solution:**
- Zero-migration integration into existing tools
- Personalized consultation process (AI-guided with human escalation)
- Three core automations that deliver immediate value (phone calls, email, calendar)
- Clear ROI demonstration through savings simulation
- Daily positive metrics visibility

**Resource Requirements:**
- **Team Size**: 3-5 developers + 1 PM + 1 designer
- **Timeline**: 4-6 months for MVP development
- **Critical Path**: Landing page → Consultation → Demo → Integration → Dashboard

**MVP Success Criteria:**
- 5 clients signed and onboarded within 3-month validation period
- 100% client satisfaction (all clients score ≥ 8/10)
- 100% ROI achievement (all clients save at least one salary equivalent)
- 100% renewal rate after 3 months
- 99%+ uptime
- 95%+ task success rate
